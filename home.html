WELCOME !

Located in the The Hague, the Netherlands, the Dancing Doll website is about dolls that come to life whenever the owner plays some music. They dance in all kind of style, such as: ballet, tap, jazz, javanese and hip-hop on the level of beginners, advanced or it falls somewhere in-between.

DD believes that a personal approach is the key to providing the best dancing performances. That's why the owner teaches the dolls herself. Her goal is not only to provide the best quality dance performance, but to help build self-assurance and confidence through positive reinforcement.She is driven to instruct her dolls to become the dancer of their dreams.

DD is pleased to welcome you to the Dancing Doll website. If you have any questions, please contact us.
